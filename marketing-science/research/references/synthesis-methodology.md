# Synthesis Methodology

How we turn raw research into the strategic inputs that positioning, archetyping, and
messaging consume. This file answers the operational question directly: **what,
specifically, does each downstream skill pull from research.** Codified July 2026 from
the Cora, Plus One/Every Agent, and Every master brand cycles.

---

## The bar before synthesis

Findings and interpretation never mix silently. A synthesis document has three visibly
separate layers: verified facts (cited), patterns (facts grouped, with the grouping
logic stated), and strategic reads (what we think it means — labeled as ours). A guess
phrased as a finding is the cardinal synthesis failure.

## What each downstream skill pulls

**Positioning pulls:** the whitespace verdict and claims-we-cannot-make list (from the
competitive audit); the customer's own words for the problem (from discovery interviews
— verbatim quotes, not paraphrases, because positioning language is built from customer
language); the category's dynamics (from the landscape — is the category forming,
consolidating, or commoditizing, which decides whether we name a new category or win an
existing one); and the why-now evidence (what changed in the world, cited).

**Archetyping pulls:** motivation evidence, not demographics — what interviewees say
they fear, want, and measure themselves by, quoted; observed behavior over stated
preference wherever both exist. Archetype assignment is feeling-first from this
evidence, never projected from the product's feature list.

**Messaging architecture pulls:** the objection inventory (every hesitation voiced in
interviews, ranked by frequency — these become the pillars' burdens of proof); the
proof assets available (stats, quotes, logos, with their clearance status); and the
enemy analysis (what the incumbent experience makes people feel).

**Brand voice pulls:** the customer's register (how they actually talk about the
problem — the voice should sound like the smartest version of the customer, not the
smartest version of us) and the category's tonal defaults (which we then deliberately
break or keep, but on purpose).

## Synthesis mechanics

Work from evidence tables, not memory: every strategic read links back to the specific
findings that earned it. Contradictory evidence is surfaced, not resolved silently —
"interviews say X, behavior data says Y" is a finding in itself. A synthesis that only
confirms the brief that scoped it gets a second, adversarial pass.

## The deliverable

A synthesis memo: one page of strategic reads, each with its evidence links; the three
input packages above (positioning / archetyping / messaging) as appendices; and a
plainly named "what we still don't know" section. Downstream skills cite the memo, not
raw research — the memo is the interface.
