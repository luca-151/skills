## Verified findings

- Interruption every 2 min core hours ("275/day" needs top-20%-of-users qualifier); 117 emails + 153 Teams messages daily; 58 messages outside core hours (+15% YoY); 57% of meetings ad hoc; after-8pm meetings +16%; ~60% communicating vs. creating; 85% of emails read in under 15 seconds; 48% say work feels chaotic; 80% lack time/energy — Microsoft Work Trend Index ("Breaking Down the Infinite Workday" + "Frontier Firm"), 2025, M365 telemetry + Edelman DxI survey n=31,000/31 markets. Primary read.
- ~1,200 app toggles/day; ~4 hrs/week reorienting — HBR, "How Much Time and Energy Do We Waste Toggling Between Applications?", Aug 2022, n=137 users/20 teams/3 Fortune 500 firms. Primary read. Small sample — flag inline when used.
- ~10 apps/day — Asana Anatomy of Work Index, 2022. (Circulating "11+ apps, Asana/Qatalog 2024" failed verification — do not ship.)
- 77 of the Fortune 100 use Slack — Slack About page, checked July 2026. Attribute to Slack; blog version ties 77 to Slack Connect.
- 42% of role knowledge known only to the person in it; 5 hrs/week waiting on the knowledge-holder; 60% find getting essential info from colleagues difficult or nearly impossible — Panopto Workplace Knowledge and Productivity Report, 2018, w/ YouGov, n≈1,000 US employees. Vendor survey, verified via secondary coverage only, 2018-vintage — internal use fine, external use needs a ruling.
- 11 hrs/week saved individually but only 13% say company performance significantly improved; 60% shuffle the same prompts between tools — Glean Work AI Institute survey, 2025, n=6,000 digital workers, US/UK/AU.
- 46.5% bounce between 2+ AI tools per task — ClickUp AI Sprawl Survey, June 2025, n=1,000+ knowledge workers.
- 76% experienced ≥1 negative outcome from disconnected AI; 30% wasting money on redundant AI software; 28% use 10+ AI apps — Zapier AI Sprawl Survey (Centiment), Oct 2025, n=550 US execs at 1,000+ employee companies.
- 88% use AI regularly; ~6% high performers (5%+ EBIT); 39% report enterprise EBIT impact; workflow redesign strongest correlate of impact — McKinsey, The State of AI: Agents, Innovation, and Transformation, Nov 2025, n=1,993, 105 countries. Primary read.
- 34% deeply transforming; 85% expect to customize agents; 21% have mature agent governance; 66% report productivity gains vs. 20% growing revenue — Deloitte, State of AI in the Enterprise: The Untapped Edge, 2026 ed. (fielded Aug–Sep 2025), n=3,235 director-to-C-suite, 24 countries. Primary read.
