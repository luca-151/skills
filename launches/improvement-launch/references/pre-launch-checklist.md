# Pre-Launch Checklist — Improvement Launch

The readiness check before an improvement launch proceeds. Each section must be complete
before moving to launch day. If any item is marked incomplete, either resolve it or make
an explicit decision to proceed without it.

This checklist is structural. Specific items within sections will be populated as we run
real improvement launches and identify what has actually caused problems. Placeholder
sections note where specifics are needed.

---

## Positioning and messaging

- [ ] Launch narrative paragraph written and approved
- [ ] The improvement is described consistently across all channel assets
- [ ] The description of the improvement is accurate — product and marketing have aligned
      on what was actually shipped and what it does
- [ ] No asset overstates what the improvement does

*[To be populated: specific messaging red lines for improvement-tier announcements,
based on actual launch retrospectives.]*

## Asset readiness

- [ ] All in-scope channel assets are final (not just drafted — final)
- [ ] All assets have been read by at least one person who did not write them
- [ ] Subject lines and headlines have been reviewed separately from body copy
- [ ] All links in assets are live and point to the right destinations
- [ ] Any product screenshots or visuals are current and accurate

## Distribution readiness

- [ ] Subscriber segments identified and confirmed
- [ ] Email send is configured correctly (list, from name, reply-to)
- [ ] Social accounts are logged in and ready
- [ ] Scheduled sends are confirmed if scheduling ahead

## Go / No-Go

- Make an explicit decision: resolve before launch, or proceed with the gap noted
- If proceeding with a gap, note who made the call and what the acceptable risk is
